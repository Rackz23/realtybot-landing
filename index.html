Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding skills to install?

Absolutely not. RealtyBot provides a tiny JavaScript snippet that you simply copy & paste into your website footer. If you use WordPress, Wix, or Squarespace, you can add the code in a custom HTML block. It takes under 2 minutes.

Is there a free trial or a money-back guarantee?

Yes—every new account gets a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. If you’re not 100% satisfied within 30 days, we offer a no-questions-asked, 100% money-back guarantee.

How secure is my data?

We encrypt all chat transcripts and lead data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Your data is never shared with third parties. We are fully GDPR and CCPA compliant.

What if I already use a different lead-capture form?

No problem—you can run RealtyBot in parallel. Our widget captures the same info (name, email, phone, budget) but does it conversationally, which dramatically increases conversion. You can also embed our form behind a “Get Demo” button.
